Join the Philongo Beta

Get early access to Philongo on Android. Help us improve the app before the public launch by testing new features and sharing your feedback.

1

Join the testers group

Join our Google Group with your Google account. This is required by Google Play for closed beta access.

2

Accept the beta invitation

Open the beta opt-in page and accept the invitation. Use the same Google account you used in step 1.

3

Download from Google Play

After accepting, Philongo will appear on the Google Play Store. Download and start learning!

It may take a few minutes after joining the group for the beta invitation link to become active. If the Play Store page says the app is unavailable, wait a few minutes and try again.
